Luxury details, aggressive stance

This idea shows both sides of the build: the clean premium feel of a Volvo and the stronger road presence that makes it stand out.

Luxury details are the quiet parts of the car that make it feel special: the shape of the lights, the clean body lines, the reflections in the paint, and the calm confidence of the design.

Aggressive stance is the stronger side. It is how the car sits on the road, how wide it feels in a shot, and how the wheels, height, angle, and lighting make the build look more serious.

The reason these two ideas work together is because contrast makes the build interesting. If a car is only aggressive, it can lose the premium feeling. If it is only luxurious, it can look too ordinary for car content.

S_XC60s lives in the middle. The cars should look refined, but not soft. They should look bold, but not messy. That balance gives the page its style.
